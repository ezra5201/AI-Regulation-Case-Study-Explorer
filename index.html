Key Colorado SB24-205 Requirements Triggered

1. Initial Deployment

  • Developer obligation to provide documentation (§6-1-1702(2))
  • Deployer requirement to implement risk management policy (§6-1-1703(2))
  • Impact assessment requirement (§6-1-1703(3))

2. Wellness Non-Compliance

  • High-risk AI system collecting sensitive health data (§6-1-1701(9))
  • Ongoing monitoring requirements (§6-1-1703(3)(g))

3. Adverse Employment Decision

  • Consequential decision affecting employment (§6-1-1701(3)(b))
  • Consumer right to human review of decisions (§6-1-1703(4)(b))
  • Notice requirements before consequential decisions (§6-1-1703(4)(a))

4. Discovery of Bias

  • Algorithmic discrimination identification (§6-1-1701(1))
  • Mandatory reporting to Attorney General (§6-1-1703(7))
  • Developer notification to deployers (§6-1-1702(5))

5. Resolution

  • Opportunity to correct personal data (§6-1-1703(4)(b)(II))
  • Appeal via human review (§6-1-1703(4)(b)(III))
  • Implementation of reasonable care standards (§6-1-1702(1) and §6-1-1703(1))
